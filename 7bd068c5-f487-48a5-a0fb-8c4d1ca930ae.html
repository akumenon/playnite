Description

In Twilight Town, you can play as either Ikuro or the Girl as you follow a funny yet engrossing storyline about giving Ikuro's AI wife, known as Sei, a real body in this first person shooter game.

Detailed blood textures and enemy limbs will keep you engaged in the combat, the enemy limbs will always land randomly to ensure no 2 kills feel quite the same.

Get up close and personal with a shotgun, shred them with a submachine gun, pick them off with a carbine or blow them to bits with explosives, coupled with a unique headshot system, boss fights and enemy variety, the combat will always manage to stay fresh to the end.

Use the game's unique parry mechanic to deflect enemy attacks back at them, furthermore, you can also stop time and unleash hell upon your foes.
