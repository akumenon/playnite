Description

Bomb the garden, smash the crockery, slam the door on your enemy, turn out the lights - do anything within your power to destroy, and survive!

Take the side of the Allies or Axis and bring your model plane to life in bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchens, and more! With 17 authentic World War II planes to choose from and online warfare, Airfix Dogfighter delivers the kind of fun metel, plastic, and glue could only dream!
