Description

Two engines. One champion. No limits.

Climb on, strap in, and experience the pure adrenaline-pumping excitement of the Pod racing sequence from Star Wars: Episode I - The Phantom Menace.

Take the controls as Anakin Skywalker or any one of over 21 Podracers, and feel the full-force blast of two massive jet engines at simulated speeds of up to 600MPH. Rein in a turbine-driven chariot through 8 spectacular worlds. Negotiate through flaming methane lakes, Tusken Raider assaults, anti-gravity tunnels and much more in a pulse-punding, do-or-die fight to the finish where anyone will do anything to cross the finish line. How far will you go?
