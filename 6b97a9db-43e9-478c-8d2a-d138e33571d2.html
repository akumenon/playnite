Description

Witness the events preceding the story of Diluvian Ultra. Embody a young Diluvian Knight, Qasar, and face the brutal Myrmidon invasion of your tomb-ship. Find and protect the sarcophagus of Attila, your Lord, and ensure he survives to set in motion the events of Diluvian Ultra.

Featuring a brand-new level, new characters, story and dialogues, Diluvian Ultra: Awakening is an excellent introduction for new explorers of the game's universe, as well as an interesting new addition for veterans.
