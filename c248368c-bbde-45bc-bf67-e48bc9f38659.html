Description

Relive your childhood! Build your army, create battle scenarios, watch the toys battle each other, and customize your game in the toy box. Command your army to fight strategically and keep them alive during battle!

Attack on Toys is a 3rd-person shooter mixed with combination of real-time strategy as toy army soldiers. Pick your favourite army colour and ride to battle! Use tanks, helicopters, trucks, and even mechs to build your own battle force to win against other toys.
